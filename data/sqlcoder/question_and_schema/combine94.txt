Count the number of distinct web sales order numbers, calculate the total shipping cost, and compute the total net profit for the period from February 1, 2000, to April 1, 2000 (a 60-day duration). This analysis focuses on customers located in the state of Oklahoma, purchasing from the web site "pri." The query excludes orders that have been returned and includes only those orders that were shipped from multiple warehouses. The results are ordered by the count of distinct web sales order numbers, and the query returns a maximum of 100 rows.
