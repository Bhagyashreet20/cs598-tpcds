Compute the total revenue and the ratio of total revenue to revenue by item class for item categories 'Shoes', 'Books', and 'Women' within the time period from January 26, 2002, to February 25, 2002. This information is grouped by item ID, item description, category, class, and current price, and sorted in ascending order based on category, class, item ID, item description, and revenue ratio. The result set is limited to the top 100 records.
