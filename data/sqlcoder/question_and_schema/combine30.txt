Find customers and their detailed customer data who have returned items purchased on the web in the state of Indiana in the year 2002. Include customers' IDs, salutations, first names, last names, preferred customer flags, birth dates, birth months, birth years, birth countries, login information, email addresses, last review dates, and the total amount they've returned. The total amount returned by each customer must be 20% higher than the average return amount of customers in the same state and time period.

The results should be ordered by customer data, specifically by customer ID, salutation, first name, last name, preferred customer flag, birth day, birth month, birth year, birth country, login, email address, last review date, and the total return amount. Finally, limit the output to the top 100 records.
