List all items (i_item_id), item descriptions (i_item_desc), and current prices (i_current_price) that were sold through the catalog channel. These items are from specific manufacturers (manufacturer IDs 856, 707, 1000, and 747) and have a current price between $45 and $75 (a $30 price range). Additionally, these items had a consistent quantity on hand between 100 and 500 during a 60-day period, specifically from February 21, 1999, to 60 days later. The results are grouped by item ID (i_item_id), item description (i_item_desc), and current price (i_current_price) and ordered by item ID (i_item_id). A maximum of 100 rows are displayed.

CREATE TABLE item (  i_item_sk,  i_item_id,  i_rec_start_date,  i_rec_end_date,  i_item_desc,  i_current_price,  i_wholesale_cost,  i_brand_id,  i_brand,  i_class_id,  i_class,  i_category_id,  i_category,  i_manufact_id,  i_manufact,  i_size,  i_formulation,  i_color,  i_units,  i_container,  i_manager_id,  i_product_name );
