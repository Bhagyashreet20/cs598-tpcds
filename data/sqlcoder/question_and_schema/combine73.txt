Count the number of customers with specific buy potentials and whose dependent count to vehicle count ratio is larger than 1 and who, in three consecutive years (2000, 2001, and 2002), made purchases in stores located in four counties (Fairfield County, Walker County, Daviess County, and Barrow County) between 1 and 5 items in one purchase. Only purchases made in the first 2 days of the months are considered. The result is sorted by the count of items in descending order and then by customer last name in ascending order.
