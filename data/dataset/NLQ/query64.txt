Find those stores that sold more cross-sales items from the year 2001 to the year 2002. Cross-sale items are items that are sold over the Internet, by catalog, and in store. The stores are identified by their names, store ZIP codes, billing and shipping street numbers and names, cities, and ZIP codes. For each store, the query provides the product name, sales year (2001), and the counts of cross-sale items sold in both years. Additionally, it includes sales information for each year, such as the wholesale cost, list price, and coupon amount for the cross-sale items. The results are ordered by product name, store name, item count in the second year, wholesale cost in the first year, and wholesale cost in the second year.
