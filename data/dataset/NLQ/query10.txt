Count the customers who share the same gender, marital status, education status, purchase estimate, credit rating, dependent count, employed dependent count, and college dependent count. This count includes customers living in specific counties, and who made purchases from both stores and another sales channel during a three-month period in a given year. The results are sorted by gender, marital status, education status, purchase estimate, credit rating, dependent count, employed dependent count, and college dependent count, and limited to the top 100 customers.
