Calculate the ratio of items sold with promotions to the total items sold in the month of November 1999. This calculation is based on the sum of sales prices for items categorized as 'Jewelry' and sold to customers residing in a time zone with a GMT offset of -7. The result is expressed as a percentage. The query first calculates the sum of sales prices for items with promotions and then calculates the sum of total sales prices. Finally, it orders the results by the sum of promotional sales prices and the sum of total sales prices in ascending order, limiting the output to the top 100 results.
