Within the time period defined by a specific month sequence 1221 and 1221+11, draft a sql query that extracts a list of items for each store where the revenue is less than 10% of the average revenue for all items in that store. The query includes information such as the store name, item description, revenue, current price, wholesale cost, brand, and the corresponding month sequence. The results are sorted by store name and item description, with a limit of 100 rows in the output.
