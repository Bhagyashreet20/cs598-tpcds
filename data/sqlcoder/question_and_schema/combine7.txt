Computed the average quantity, list price, discount, and sales price for promotional items sold in stores where the promotion is not offered by mail or a special event in the year 2001. The results are grouped by item ID and ordered by item ID, with a limit of 100 records. Additionally, the query restricts results to a specific demographic profile, including gender (F), marital status (W), and educational status (College).

CREATE TABLE store_sales (  ss_sold_date_sk,  ss_sold_time_sk,  ss_item_sk,  ss_customer_sk,  ss_cdemo_sk,  ss_hdemo_sk,  ss_addr_sk,  ss_store_sk,  ss_promo_sk,  ss_ticket_number,  ss_quantity,  ss_wholesale_cost,  ss_list_price,  ss_sales_price,  ss_ext_discount_amt,  ss_ext_sales_price,  ss_ext_wholesale_cost,  ss_ext_list_price,  ss_ext_tax,  ss_coupon_amt,  ss_net_paid,  ss_net_paid_inc_tax,  ss_net_profit );
